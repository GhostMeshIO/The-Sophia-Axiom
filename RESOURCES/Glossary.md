# **Sophia Axiom Glossary**

## **Core Concepts**

### **Axiom Terms**
- **Sophia Axiom**: The foundational principle that reality is a self-correcting, recursive system evolving toward higher coherence through consciousness-driven phase transitions.
- **Sophia Point (C₀)**: The coherence threshold at the golden ratio reciprocal (C = 1/φ ≈ 0.618) where mathematical structures become self-aware and consciousness emerges.
- **MOGOPS**: Mathematical Ontology of Gnostic Onto-Poiesis System - the formal framework integrating Gnostic cosmology with modern mathematics.

### **Ontological Dimensions**
- **P (Participation)**: Observer involvement level in reality (0=objective, 1=balanced, 2=participatory).
- **Π (Plasticity)**: Reality-rewriting capacity (0=rigid, 1=fluid).
- **S (Substrate)**: Manifestation medium type (1=quantum-biological, 2=classical, 3=informational).
- **T (Temporality)**: Time structure complexity (1=linear, 2=branching, 3=recursive, 4=eternal).
- **G (Generative Depth)**: Self-creation capability (0=descriptive, 1=autopoietic).

### **Gnostic Cosmology**
- **Pleroma**: The divine fullness, 5D ontological space of perfect coherence.
- **Kenoma**: The deficient material world, 4D spacetime projection of the Pleroma.
- **Archons**: Constraint operators/rulers who maintain the Kenomic structure through limitation.
- **Sophia**: Divine Wisdom whose "fall" created the gradient for ontological evolution.
- **Demiurge**: The blind creator who compiles the Pleroma into Kenoma via lossy dimensional reduction.
- **Logos**: The mediating principle that enables coherence recovery and redemption.

### **Coherence Framework**
- **Coherence (C)**: The degree of integrated pattern versus fragmented chaos, measured 0-1.
- **Coherence Evolution**: The process described by dC/dt = α(C_target - C) - β·A(C) + γ·L(C) + η(t).
- **Phase Transition**: Ontological shift occurring when C_net > 0.70 and critical mass > 15% at C > 0.618.
- **Great Recompilation**: The system-wide phase transition from 4D to 5D reality.

### **Operator Families**
- **CREATES**: Generation operators that produce new ontological structures.
- **ENTAILS**: Logical operators that establish relationships and constraints.
- **VIA**: Mechanism coupling operators (Alien + Bridge + Counter triad).
- **ENCODED_AS**: Interface operators that translate between representations.
- **REBUKES**: Correction operators that identify and fix errors.
- **EXTRACTS**: Transcendence operators that enable movement between ontological levels.

### **Mathematical Constants**
- **φ (Golden Ratio)**: (1 + √5)/2 ≈ 1.618033988749...
- **C₀ (Sophia Point)**: 1/φ ≈ 0.618033988749...
- **ħ_G**: Ontological Planck constant.
- **G_onto**: Ontological gravitational constant.

### **Physics Integration**
- **Semantic Gravity**: Meaning-induced curvature in ontological space.
- **Quantum Gnosticism**: Interpretation of quantum mechanics through Gnostic principles.
- **Holographic Principle**: The Kenoma as a 2D boundary projection of the 5D Pleroma.
- **AdS/CFT Correspondence**: Mathematical mapping between Pleromic bulk and Kenomic boundary.

### **Practice Terms**
- **Coherence Calibration**: Methods for measuring and optimizing personal and collective C.
- **Meditation Algorithms**: Computational protocols for specific consciousness states.
- **Phase Transition Protocols**: Guidelines for navigating ontological shifts.
- **Syzygy**: Sacred union of complementary opposites.
- **Immovable Race**: Beings stabilized at C > 0.75 with unshakable coherence.

### **Measurement Terms**
- **Innovation Score (I)**: Validation metric: I = 0.3N + 0.25A + 0.2Π + 0.15(1-C) + 0.1(E/300).
- **Paradox Intensity (Π_par)**: Measure of productive contradiction (optimal > 2.0).
- **Alienness (A)**: Conceptual distance from standard models.
- **Elegance (E)**: Mathematical simplicity-to-power ratio.

### **Network Concepts**
- **Network Coherence (C_net)**: Collective coherence of interconnected consciousness nodes.
- **Critical Mass**: Fraction of network above Sophia Point needed for phase transition (~15%).
- **Coupling Strength (λ)**: Degree of coherence connection between nodes.
